# Information Diet

An information diet is the deliberate curation of what you read, watch, and consume.

Just as physical diet shapes the body, the information diet shapes thought.
Consuming low-quality information crowds out space for deeper thinking.

## Principles

- **Slow news**: prefer analysis over breaking updates.
- **Primary sources**: read the original paper, not the summary of the summary.
- **Depth over breadth**: one well-understood concept beats ten half-remembered ones.
